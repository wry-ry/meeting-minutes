## 4. Open Discussions & TC PR Reviews

- **Topic:** Schema Versioning & Redirects
  - **Discussion:** Ryan Cheung proposed cleaning up unversioned JSON schema artifacts.
  - **Decision:** Unversioned schemas will point to the latest published version via 302 redirects.
- **Topic:** Catalog Capability for Product Discovery
  - **Discussion:** Ilya Grigorik is working on open comments. No additional input needed from TC at this time.
  - **Decision:** Approved to land in draft.
  - **Context:** [PR #55 Catalog Search+Lookup capabilities for product discovery](https://github.com/Universal-Commerce-Protocol/ucp/pull/55)
- **Topic:** Loyalty and Identity Modeling
  - **Discussion:** Anurag provided an update on loyalty modeling. The council debated whether loyalty should be tied to identity or treated as a separate lifecycle. Etsy, Target, and Shopify shared their respective models.
  - **Decision:** Anurag will share a PR next week. The focus will be on defining high-level constructs for benefits applied during checkout.
- **Topic:** Itemized Fees Extension
  - **Discussion:** Maxime raised the need for multiple fee types (e.g., bagging, road improvement) with descriptions.
  - **Decision:** Fees will be modeled as an extension similar to discounts.
  - **Context:** [PR #220 Add itemized fee extension for checkout and cart](https://github.com/Universal-Commerce-Protocol/ucp/pull/220) (Resolves Issue #219 Clarify itemized fee support in totals)
- **Topic:** Payment Instrument Qualifiers
  - **Discussion:** Discussed whether to bake max length/items constraints into the schema.
  - **Decision:** These will be treated as implementation details. Recommended limits will be documented in a new "Implementation Requirements" section at the bottom of the spec.
  - **Context:** [PR #214 feat: add payment instrument qualifiers](https://github.com/Universal-Commerce-Protocol/ucp/pull/214) (Resolves Issue #137 Allow tender-based discounts via payment instrument qualifiers in Checkout Session Update)
- **Topic:** Version Negotiation
  - **Discussion:** Ilya updated the version negotiation PR to address gaps identified during implementation, including access to versioned profiles.
  - **Decision:** Anurag will review from an implementation standpoint.
  - **Context:** [PR #200 Clarify version negotiation](https://github.com/Universal-Commerce-Protocol/ucp/pull/200)
- **Topic:** Preferred Ordering of Payment
  - **Discussion:** Discussed the ability for merchants to suggest a display order for payment instruments.
  - **Decision:** Drew Olson is reviewing platform policy regarding steering vs. merchant ordering. For discussion next week.
  - **Context:** [Issue #170 Allow merchant to specify preferred ordering of payment](https://github.com/Universal-Commerce-Protocol/ucp/issues/170)
